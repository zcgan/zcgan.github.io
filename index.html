Welcome to My Homepage

I am currently a postdoctoral assistant professor at Department of Mathematics, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am working with Prof. Robert Krasny on applied math and scientfic computing, with application in soft condensed matter physics, material science and biology.

I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Institute of Natural Sciences and Department of Mathematics, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhenli Xu. I obtained my bachlor degree in Mechanical Engineering from School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
